Italian Comfort: Classic Lasagne Recipe

Lasagna, a quintessential Italian comfort food, is a culinary masterpiece characterized by its rich layers of pasta, savory sauce, and creamy cheese. The dish typically begins with sheets of flat pasta, traditionally made from durum wheat semolina, creating a sturdy foundation for the subsequent layers. Each layer is meticulously crafted, with generous spreads of tomato-based sauce infused with herbs and spices, intertwining with creamy ricotta and grated Parmesan cheese. These layers harmonize to create a symphony of flavors, each bite offering a delightful combination of textures and tastes.

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ragu
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion, garlic and basil
  • Salt and pepper (seasoning to your taste)
