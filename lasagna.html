Italian Comfort: Classic Lasagne Recipe

Lasagna, a quintessential Italian comfort food, is a culinary masterpiece characterized by its rich layers of pasta, savory sauce, and creamy cheese. The dish typically begins with sheets of flat pasta, traditionally made from durum wheat semolina, creating a sturdy foundation for the subsequent layers. Each layer is meticulously crafted, with generous spreads of tomato-based sauce infused with herbs and spices, intertwining with creamy ricotta and grated Parmesan cheese. These layers harmonize to create a symphony of flavors, each bite offering a delightful combination of textures and tastes.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ragu
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion, garlic and basil
  • Salt and pepper (seasoning to your taste)

How to prepare:

Sauce

  1. If using ground beef or sausage, cook it in a skillet over medium heat until browned. Remove excess grease.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.
  3. Pour in the tomato sauce and season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes to let the flavors meld.

Prepare the Cheese Mixture

  1. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with some grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt, pepper, and any additional herbs if desired. Mix well.

Assemble the Lasagna

  1. Prehear the oven to 190Â°C.
  2. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a baking dish to prevent sticking.
  3. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  4. Spread a portion of the ricotta mixture evenly over the noodles.
  5. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese on top of the ricotta mixture.
  6. Spoon a layer of the meat sauce over the cheese.
  7. Repeat the process, ensuring that the top layer is sauce-covered and sprinkled with a generous amount of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover the top with aluminum foil and place it in the oven for 25-30 min.
  9. Remove the aluminum foil and let it cook until the cheese is golden brown
  10. Let it rest for about 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving
  11. Serve your delicious lasagna slices with a sprinkle of extra Parmesan cheese and perhaps some fresh herbs on top if desired.
